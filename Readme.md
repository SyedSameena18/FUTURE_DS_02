# Customer Retention and Churn Analysis

## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer churn behavior in a telecom subscription-based business. The objective is to identify key factors influencing customer retention and customer loss through data analysis and visualization techniques.

The analysis helps businesses understand churn patterns and make data-driven decisions to improve customer loyalty and reduce customer attrition.

## Project Objectives
- Analyze customer churn trends
- Identify high-risk customer segments
- Understand factors affecting customer retention
- Generate actionable business insights
- Recommend strategies to reduce customer churn

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

The project includes analysis of:

### 1. Customer Churn Distribution
Analyzed the overall churn rate among customers.

### 2. Contract Type vs Churn
Identified churn behavior across different contract types.

### 3. Payment Method vs Churn
Studied the relationship between payment methods and customer churn.

### 4. Internet Service vs Churn
Examined churn patterns among different internet service users.

### 5. Customer Tenure Analysis
Analyzed how customer tenure impacts retention behavior.


## Key Business Insights
- Customers with month-to-month contracts show the highest churn rate.
- Electronic check payment users demonstrate higher churn behavior.
- Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to leave the service.
- Long-term contracts positively influence customer retention.
- Fiber optic internet users display relatively higher churn patterns.


## Business Recommendations
1. Introduce loyalty rewards and discounts for long-term subscriptions.
2. Encourage customers to shift from monthly plans to yearly contracts.
3. Improve customer support for fiber optic users.
4. Promote automatic payment methods to improve retention.
5. Implement targeted retention campaigns for newly acquired customers.
